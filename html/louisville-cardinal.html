Student and faculty participated in the 20th anniversary of the University of Louisville LBGT center's drag show PINK. The show helps support scholarships for LGBTQ students as well as create an inclusive community. “Every year, PINK is a powerful showing of the support that people at U of L, whether it be the students, faculty or staff, have for the LGBT community,” program coordinator Katy Garrison said. “It is a chance for students to express themselves and feel supported through that journey.”
